Scuderia Ferrari

Description

The grandest and most historical name in Formula 1, Scuderia Ferrari have been a part of the sport since its inception in 1950. The Italian team are unparalleled and have won 15 drivers' championships and 16 constructors’ titles, while they also hold the records of most wins, most points, most podiums and most one-two’s finishes.

Any young driver aspires to drive for the Ferrari F1 team. Their history, their grandeur and their success has given them a mystical status within the sport, and drivers such as Fangio, Lauda, Prost and Schumacher have added to their global appeal and reputation. The team is named after their founder, Enzo Ferrari, while 'Scuderia' is an Italian term for a stable kept for racing horses, hence the teams nickname ‘the prancing horse’.
